Essential Residential Moving Checklist

Moving your business can be a breeze with the right planning! At Comfort Movers, we’re excited to guide you through every step and make your move smooth and stress-free.

Evaluate the new location for its impact on employee commutes and operational needs.

Collaborate with your moving company to create a detailed, step-by-step plan for a smooth transition.

Make a comprehensive inventory of all items to be moved to track belongings and avoid loss.

Visit the new site to plan item placement for efficient unpacking and setup.

Inform utilities, ISPs, vendors, and customers about the move to prevent disruptions.

Create backups of critical data and establish security measures for sensitive information and equipment.
